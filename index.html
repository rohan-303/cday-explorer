C-Day Explorer

10 Years of KSU Computing Innovation

An interactive visualization of a decade of capstone projects, research, and innovation from Kennesaw State University's College of Computing and Software Engineering. Explore 1,286 projects across 11 domains spanning 21 semesters of C-Day showcases.

Methodology

  • Data collected from 21 semesters of C-Day archives (2016–2025)
  • Abstracts extracted from Digital Commons and archive PDFs
  • 631 poster PDF links and 345 YouTube presentation videos matched
  • 223 award winners identified from event pages and program archives
  • AI-powered "Take It Further" suggestions generated for every project
  • NLP-based keyword extraction and project similarity analysis
